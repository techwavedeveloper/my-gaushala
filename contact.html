Frequently Asked Questions

How can I donate?
You can donate through our website by clicking the "Donate Now" button or by visiting our dedicated donate page. We accept online transfers and direct contributions.
Can I visit the sanctuary?
Yes! We welcome visitors. Please contact us at +91 9811319139 to schedule a visit. Visiting hours are 10 AM to 5 PM on weekdays and weekends.
How is my donation used?
100% of donations go towards cow care including food, medical treatment, shelter, and daily operations. We maintain complete transparency with regular reports.
Can I sponsor a specific cow?
Absolutely! Many donors choose to sponsor specific cows they meet. Contact us to learn about our cow sponsorship program and meet your adopted cow.
Do you provide donation receipts?
Yes, we provide detailed receipts for all donations. Tax-exempt certificates are also available for eligible donors.
How can I volunteer?
We welcome volunteers! You can help with care, medical support, feeding, and sanctuary maintenance. Contact us to learn about volunteer opportunities.
