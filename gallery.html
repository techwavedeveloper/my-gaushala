Rescue Stories

Gauri's Journey

Found: Abandoned on the streets with injuries

Story: Gauri was rescued from the busy roads of Noida in critical condition. After 3 months of intensive veterinary care, she has fully recovered and now enjoys peaceful days in our sanctuary.

Rescue Success

Radha's Recovery

Found: Injured with a broken leg

Story: Radha arrived unable to walk. Our veterinarians performed surgery and provided months of rehabilitation. Today, she runs and plays with other cows.

Lakshmi's New Life

Found: Malnourished and neglected

Story: Lakshmi was severely malnourished when rescued. With proper nutrition and care, she has transformed into a healthy, energetic cow loved by all.
