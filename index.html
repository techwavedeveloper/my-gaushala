Our Mission

Rescuing, Protecting, and Nurturing Cows

In Indian culture, the cow is revered as 'Gau Mata'. However, many are left abandoned on the streets, injured, or starving. Our Goshala provides a sanctuary for these innocent souls, offering them love, care, and a dignified life.
