About Our Gau Seva Mission

Protecting, Nurturing, and Serving Sacred Cows

Our Story

Neem Karori Baba Gau Seva, operating under Sammaan Foundation, is dedicated to the protection, welfare, and care of abandoned and injured cows in Greater Noida. Founded with the belief that cows hold a sacred place in Indian culture and society, our mission is to provide sanctuary for these gentle beings.

Every cow deserves dignity, love, and proper care. We rescue cows from the streets, provide medical treatment, nutritious food, and a safe home where they can live peacefully.
